How it works

Every time your compliance policy or underwriting guidelines change, PolicyProof detects it, versions it, timestamps it, and shows you exactly what constraint mutated — line by line.
02 / VERIFY

Every AI decision is tagged with the policy version active at that exact moment. If the AI used a stale version, the system flags it instantly — before it reaches an auditor.
